Why are we making a website?

Express yourself

Show off your unique personality, interests, and talents to the world. Whatever you're passionate about – art, music, sports, or science – showcase what makes you special.

Share your ideas and opinions

A place to share your thoughts, opinions, and ideas with others

Learn valuable skills

The entire web runs on HTML and CSS (plus Javascript and other things).

This is a great way to learn about these important parts of web design, and have some fun, sharing your ideas, along the way.
